Use Case 06

Passenger connection risk before the gate becomes the failure point.

Athenalytica helps airlines detect itineraries most likely to break under disruption so recovery action can happen earlier, with better passenger and operational outcomes.

Connection risk boardItinerary vulnerability, passenger value, and rebooking feasibility in one service-recovery view.

Guest recovery context

Missed connections carry more than operational cost. They affect loyalty, call volume, airport congestion, and brand trust. The right response depends on seeing risk early enough to act.

Typical challenge

Connection risk is often treated after the operating event is already unfolding, which narrows re-accommodation options and worsens guest experience.

  • Hub flows create uneven vulnerability across banks and connection windows
  • Passenger value and itinerary complexity are not always visible together
  • Service teams need proactive priorities, not just delay notifications

Athenalytica approach

The use case scores onward itineraries by timing, disruption exposure, rebooking feasibility, and customer importance so teams can intervene deliberately.

  • Connection-risk scoring with itinerary sensitivity factors
  • Passenger segmentation linked to service-recovery urgency
  • Rebooking probability and alternative path visibility

Outcome lenses

Guest service becomes a measurable operations discipline rather than a reactive catch-up process.
