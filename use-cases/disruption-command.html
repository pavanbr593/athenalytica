Use Case 02

Disruption command before delay becomes network damage.

Athenalytica gives operations teams a live recovery view that connects aircraft, station, crew, and passenger pressure so the next move is based on consequence, not only sequence.

Recovery command boardDelay propagation, connection stress, and reserve posture tracked in one disruption surface.

What the use case solves

During irregular operations, teams need a common language for recovery. The main problem is not missing data. It is fragmented data arriving without a shared priority model.

Operational challenge

Control centers often have to choose between short-term on-time recovery and downstream passenger or crew consequences.

  • Delay impacts spread differently by hub structure and bank design
  • Aircraft recovery decisions affect crew legality and reserve posture
  • Connection risk is usually surfaced too late for proactive action

Athenalytica model

The platform scores disruption events by total network consequence, not by isolated delay minutes alone.

  • Delay propagation maps with station sensitivity layers
  • Connection-risk views tied to loyalty and itinerary value
  • Crew recovery overlays for reserve and legality resilience

Command metrics

Metrics are framed for recovery teams that must protect the network under pressure.

Cascade riskEstimate which delay events are likely to generate network-wide instability.
Guest impactQuantify connection and service-recovery exposure before the gate event.
Crew stressSee where recovery options are constrained by legality or reserve limits.
Recovery valuePrioritize actions with the greatest operational and commercial benefit.

Execution flow

Recovery sequencing is designed so operations leaders can move from detection to intervention rapidly.

01Map the eventModel the disruption footprint across aircraft rotations, stations, crews, and guests.
02Rank intervention pathsCompare swaps, holds, reserve moves, and re-accommodation options by total outcome.
03Coordinate the responsePush one aligned recovery priority set across operations, crew, and customer teams.
