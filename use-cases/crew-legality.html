Use Case 03

Crew legality visibility before recovery options disappear.

Athenalytica helps crew teams see legality drift, reserve pressure, and fatigue-sensitive roster paths early enough to intervene without compounding operational risk.

Crew resilience boardLegality exposure, reserve coverage, and disruption posture surfaced together.

Why this matters

Legality issues become expensive when they are discovered late. The system has to show not just who is exposed, but where reserve and recovery flexibility is already running thin.

Planning pressure

Crew planning and day-of-operations teams often work from separate views, making legality risks harder to resolve smoothly.

  • Reserve availability changes rapidly under disruption
  • Roster drift can turn marginal duty plans into hard failures
  • Recovery actions can solve one rotation while creating another exposure

Athenalytica response

The platform scores crew resilience with legality, fatigue, reserve, and downstream schedule implications in one decision layer.

  • Reserve posture heatmaps by base, rank, and fleet
  • Legality exception forecasting before day-of-ops compromise
  • Recovery options ranked by continuity and compliance confidence

Key visibility metrics

Crew analytics should help teams act earlier, not simply document exceptions after the fact.
